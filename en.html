Say goodbye to the frustration of searching for in-network gas stations and wasting time and fuel in the process. Track every single transaction

Yeul simplifies expense management with a straightforward dashboard that allows you to monitor expenses in real-time. It eliminates the need for handling receipts with advanced expense tracking technology. You can easily assign and unassign an unlimited number of cards to drivers in seconds, ensuring complete control over expenses.

Prevent Misuse and Fraud Quickly with Yeul.

As a fleet manager, you can utilize various tools such as setting spend limits and operational hours, blocking transactions or flagging them for review, receiving instant alerts to prevent misuse and fraud, and enabling fuel-only purchases or allowing your drivers to spend on other fleet expenses to better control and manage your fleet's expenses.

Sustainable

Yeul Visa fleet card is an environmentally friendly solution for Swedish fleets. It enables fueling anywhere, reducing fuel consumption and greenhouse emission. With the option to choose biogas diesel, fleets can further reduce their carbon footprint. Yeul also supports payments for EV charging, making the transition to sustainable transportation easier, while keeping all fleet expenses in one place.

Competitive discounts

Optimize your fuel, parking, maintenance or restaurant spending with precise controls per merchant category. Earn up to 60 öre per litre at any gas station and a 1% cashback on every purchase at merchants other than gas stations, with no cap or tier.
